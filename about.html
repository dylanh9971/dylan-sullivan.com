About me

I've always loved the grit of creating things from the ground up. That drive to build naturally led me to technology and my current path in Computing and IT. Whether I'm studying for my degree at The Open University or diving into a new project, I treat coding as a craft—always refining my skills to keep my work sharp and my perspective fresh.

Beyond the screen, I'm usually found building strength or strategy; I'm an amateur arm-wrestler by day and a dedicated gamer by night.

Living in a rural area, I've seen how a lack of digital outreach can hold local small businesses back. My goal is to bridge that gap, helping them move beyond local word-of-mouth to reach neighboring towns and cities with a platform that actually works for them.
