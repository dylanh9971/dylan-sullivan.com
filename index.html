Hi, I'm Dylan

Where I'm from

Ireland born and raised. I grew up surrounded by good stories and lots of history - which somehow shaped how I approach design. I believe; good design, like a good story, needs structure, patience and a little imagination.

What I do

I'm a naturally creative person and an aspiring web developer. I specialise in UI/UX, but I dont stop at the design file - I build the front-end myself, so nothing gets lost between the sketch and the shipped product.

Outside of work

When I'm not at a screen, I'm at a table - literally. I'm an amateur arm-wrestler, competing in my spare time. Turns out "quietly obsessed with the details" applies just as much to grip technique as it does to pixel spacing.
